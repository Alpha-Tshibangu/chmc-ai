Welcome to the CHMC-AI Closed Beta v1.0

Thank you for participating in the closed beta! I'm building an Artificial Intelligence Web Application capable of simple question-answering regarding the Chisholm Medical Centre, its services, and its operations. Although it is quite simple, the AI-model responsible for serving answers to user questions will eventually be able to produce more open generative responses and fewer extractive ones. Your participation will help me to collect data on the web app's usage, including the scope of user enquiry. It also allows me to measure key server performance metrics (since right now, the web/app "server" is my little brother's GPU-equipped laptop. Please review the "Docs" (top left) on GitHub to understand more about this project and the development process. I look forward to your feedback!

Note: The web app is not suitable for smaller screen sizes (i.e. mobile) at this point in development! You could use it on your phone, but your experience will be interesting.
